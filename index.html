Why "Exploring India" Matters

India's heritage is not just in its world-famous monuments but in thousands of lesser-known sites that tell equally important stories.

Cultural Diversity

Each region of India has unique architectural styles, traditions, and histories that remain undocumented.

Historical Continuity

These sites connect us to local histories, folk traditions, and community memories that mainstream history often overlooks.

Preservation Urgency

Many heritage sites face threats from urbanization, neglect, and climate change. Digital documentation creates a lasting record.
